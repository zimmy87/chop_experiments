create a O3DE project that is a simple first-person shooter game with the following features:

-the player runs forward and backward with keys W and S
-the player strafes left and right with keys A and D
-the player's aiming can be controlled with mouse movement
-the player has a gun, represented with an elongated cube, that fires smaller cubes as bullets when pressing left mouse button
-houses made of cubes with pyramid roofs are randomly placed around the world
-the ground is a flat green plane
-enemies represented by red cubes spawn randomly around the player
-enemy behavior is to approach the player in a speed slightly slower than the player's run speed
-the player starts with 100 health
-when an enemy contacts the player, the enemy is destroyed, and the player's health decreases by 10 points
-when an enemy contacts a bullet, the enemy is destroyed
